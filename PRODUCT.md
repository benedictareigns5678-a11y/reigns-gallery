# PRODUCT.md — Suleiman Sallah, Portfolio Gallery

register: brand

## What this is
A private gallery of websites built by Suleiman Sallah. Each "work" is a live,
deployed website that opens in its own tab. Clients receive the link
(reigns-gallery.vercel.app) and browse by room (category). The gallery itself
is the proof of craft: it must read as expensive, curated, and unmistakably
designed by a person with taste.

## Users
High-paying prospective clients (founders, brand owners, marketing leads)
deciding whether to commission a website. They visit once, alone, usually on
desktop, often at night. The single job of the page: make them feel they have
been let into something private and want a piece of their own on this wall.

## Organizing idea
A private museum, after closing time. The visitor is alone, after hours.
Rooms are dim, each work hangs lit by its own spotlight, brass plaques
identify each piece, and somewhere the maker is still working. Scrolling is
walking the promenade. Hovering a work is stepping closer.
Emotional target: quiet privilege.

## Anti-references
Template portfolio grids, SaaS landing pages, dribbble-shot card walls,
purple/indigo gradients, glassmorphism, centered-hero-with-two-pill-buttons,
raw untreated screenshots, equal-box card grids, placeholder anything.

## Constraints
- Static HTML/CSS/JS, no build step. Data lives in js/works.js only.
- The owner is not a developer: works.js stays the single file they touch.
- Every work's live external link must always survive any redesign.
- Adding work No. 04 is a data entry, not a redesign.
